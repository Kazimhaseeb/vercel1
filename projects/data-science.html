A suite of data science tools focused on financial market data. The Polygon.io OHLCV Archiver bulk-downloads historical bars for thousands of symbols into structured Parquet/CSV datasets with incremental update logic. The Crypto Analytics Dashboard (Plotly Dash) provides interactive candlestick charts, volume profiles, cross-asset correlation heatmaps, and drawdown analysis for a live portfolio view.

OHLCV Archiver

  • Bulk download for 10,000+ tickers
  • Async rate-limit-aware fetching
  • Parquet + CSV dual-format output
  • Incremental updates (new bars only)
  • Symbol universe management
  • Data quality validation checks
  • Overnight scheduled runs via cron

Analytics Dashboard

  • Interactive candlestick + volume charts
  • VWAP & SMA/EMA overlays
  • Cross-asset correlation heatmap
  • Max drawdown & Sharpe ratio
  • Portfolio P&L tracking
  • Custom date range filtering
  • Real-time data refresh callbacks

EDA & Analysis Pipelines

Return Distribution Analysis: Daily/weekly/monthly return distributions, skewness, kurtosis, and fat-tail detection across asset classes.
Correlation Matrix Generator: Rolling 30/90/252-day cross-asset correlation heatmaps updated daily for portfolio risk monitoring.
Volatility Surface Plotter: 3D volatility surface charts across strikes and expiries from options chain data.
Sector Rotation Tracker: Relative strength comparison of 11 GICS sectors with momentum ranking and visual flow charts.
Drawdown Analyzer: Maximum drawdown, recovery time, underwater equity curves, and Calmar ratio computation for strategy evaluation.
Earnings Impact Study: Systematic backtesting of price moves around earnings announcements across 500+ stocks.
