Ejaz (إيجاز) is a comprehensive property management platform built for the Saudi market. The Flutter mobile app gives landlords full control over villas and apartment buildings — from onboarding and passwordless login to a live financial dashboard, per-unit rent tracking, and official Ejar lease contract generation.

Licensed for property marketing and fully integrated with Saudi Arabia's official Ejar leasing platform, Ejaz ensures every contract complies with local regulations. Landlords get real-time income/expense visibility, occupancy rates, expense categorisation, property notes, and one-tap WhatsApp communication with tenants — all from a single polished mobile experience on iOS and Android.

Core Features

Financial Dashboard — Live income, expenses, net profit, payment status cards (overdue, due, received), and monthly cash flow chart.
Property & Unit Management — Manage villas and apartment buildings with unit-level rented/vacant status and portfolio overview.
Ejar Integration — Official Saudi government leasing platform integration for legally compliant, registered lease contracts.
Payment Tracking — Per-unit rent due dates, overdue alerts, remaining balances, and one-tap record payment.
Tenant Directory — Central contact hub with contract dates, call, and WhatsApp buttons per tenant.
Expense & Notes Logs — Categorised property expenses (utilities, maintenance) and timestamped operational notes per property.

System Architecture

Mobile — Flutter

  • Single codebase for iOS & Android
  • Passwordless email authentication
  • Financial charts & occupancy UI

  • Python Flask API on AWS

Platform Highlights

Onboarding & Passwordless Login

Three-step onboarding builds trust with Ejar branding and value props. Login uses email verification codes — no traditional password required.

Home Financial Dashboard

Personalised greeting, overdue/due/received payment cards, income vs expenses summary, monthly cash flow chart, and quick-access units drawer for portfolio-wide occupancy.

Property Detail Tabs

Each property shows map, specs, and three tabs — Lease (Ejar contracts, payment status, tenant), Expenses (itemised utilities & maintenance), and Notes (operational log).

Multi-Unit Buildings

Apartment buildings get a units list with per-unit tenant assignment, plus building-level expenses and notes — identical workflow to villa properties.

Landlord Workflow

1
Onboard & Sign In
New landlords complete the three-step intro, then authenticate via passwordless email verification.
2
Review Financial Dashboard
Home screen shows payment health, cash flow, and occupancy at a glance — with quick access to all units.
3
Add & Manage Properties
Register villas or apartment buildings, specify units, and track rented vs vacant status across the portfolio.
4
Track Leases & Payments
Ejar-compliant contracts, record payments, flag overdue rent, and monitor remaining balances per unit.
5
Communicate & Stay Alerted
Message tenants via WhatsApp from the app. Firebase push notifications alert landlords to due dates and key events.
