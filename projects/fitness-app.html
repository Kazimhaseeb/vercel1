Fitness App is a comprehensive mobile application designed to connect users with personalised fitness services, promote healthier lifestyles, and provide seamless management of fitness-related activities.

With a clean and modern user interface, the app simplifies navigation while focusing on usability and practicality. It is tailored for both beginners and fitness enthusiasts, offering features that enhance user experience and ensure consistency in their fitness journey — from browsing services and booking sessions to managing profiles and receiving real-time updates.

Core Features

Secure Authentication — Login and sign-up flow with email/password access, forgot password recovery, and new user profile setup.
Services & Categories — Diverse fitness options including Bootcamps, Yoga, Assisted Stretching, and Personal Training with detailed descriptions.
Profile & Personalisation — Users manage contact info, fitness goals, progress tracking, and view trainer profiles for tailored engagement.
Booking System — Schedule sessions with trainers, book group classes, select time slots, and manage upcoming appointments.
Secure Payments — Integrated payment system for booking sessions with secure online transactions and booking management.
Notifications & Updates — Real-time alerts for appointment confirmations, trainer updates, session reminders, and rescheduling alerts.

User Workflow

1
