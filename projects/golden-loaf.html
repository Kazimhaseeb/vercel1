Golden Loaf is a mobile application tailored for bakery enthusiasts, offering a seamless and highly personalized shopping experience. Users explore, select, and purchase bakery products according to their preferences — from whole wheat and sourdough to rye and puff pastry.

With a warm and inviting interface, the app features curated recommendations, easy location management for home and office delivery, promo codes at checkout, and full account settings — making every browse and order feel effortless.

Core Features

Personal Taste Profiles — Users select bakery preferences (Whole Wheat, Sourdough, Rye, Puff Pastry) for tailored product recommendations.
Homepage & Browse — Search bar, personalised faves section, and categorised products with high-quality images.
Location Management — Save multiple delivery addresses (Home, Office) with street, city, postal code, and phone.
Cart & Checkout — Quantity adjustment, promo codes, order summary, and estimated delivery time.
Settings & Account — Profile info, saved locations, taste preferences, and favourite items in one place.
Secure Authentication — Streamlined sign-up and login with data protection for a safe onboarding experience.
