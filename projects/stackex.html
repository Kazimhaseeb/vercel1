Stackex is a comprehensive peer-to-peer precious metals brokerage platform that connects individual buyers and sellers of physical precious metals — including gold, silver, platinum, and palladium. The platform acts as a trusted broker intermediary, managing authentication, payment escrow, insured shipping, and dispute resolution between counterparties.

Unlike traditional commodity exchanges dealing in financial derivatives, Stackex facilitates the trade of physical, tangible assets. Every item listed undergoes a validation process at the platform's fulfillment warehouse before delivery to the buyer, ensuring purity, weight, and authenticity. Built on Flask & SQLAlchemy with Stripe Connect for split payment escrow and Cabrella API for insured two-leg logistics.

Core Value Proposition

Authenticated Metals — Every listed item verified for purity and weight at a certified warehouse before delivery, ensuring physical integrity.
Secure Stripe Escrow — Stripe-powered payment holds ensure funds are only released to sellers upon confirmed order completion.
Two-Leg Insured Shipping — Cabrella API powers seller → warehouse → buyer insured logistics with real-time tracking on both legs.
Live Spot Price Ticker — Homepage displays real-time market data for Gold (XAU), Silver (XAG), and Platinum (XPT) with change indicators.
Dual-Role Accounts — Users can act as both buyers and sellers, switching roles seamlessly within a single account dashboard.
Reputation System — Post-order reviews build trust profiles for both buyers and sellers, enabling a self-regulating marketplace community.

Platform Highlights

Marketplace & Listings

The marketplace displays all active listings in a filterable, sortable grid. Buyers can filter by metal category (Gold, Silver, Platinum, Palladium), min/max price, and sort by recency. Each card shows product image, category badge, weight, price, and seller info.

Cart & Dynamic Fee Calculation

The cart fetches real-time Cabrella shipping rates as users type their address, updating the order total live — product price + validation fee (Standard $49.99 / Premium $99.99) + 2% insurance + two-leg shipping. Stripe card element embedded at checkout.

Seller Earnings Dashboard

Connected sellers view live Stripe balance, pending payouts, and upcoming orders through an embedded Stripe Express panel. Admins manage orders, generate shipping labels, and monitor full tracking for both delivery legs.

Validation Tiers

Two validation levels (Standard and Premium) give buyers control over quality assurance depth. Standard ($49.99/item) covers basic authenticity and weight. Premium ($99.99/item) includes full spectrographic purity analysis before dispatch.

Order & Transaction Flow

1
Seller Lists Item
Seller creates a listing with images, metal category, weight, purity, and asking price. Items appear in the marketplace immediately.
2
Buyer Adds to Cart & Checks Out
Buyer selects validation tier, enters shipping address, receives real-time Cabrella shipping rates, and pays via Stripe with full fee transparency.
3
Payment Escrow Activated
Stripe PaymentIntent holds buyer funds in escrow. Platform application fee is captured. Seller is notified to ship to the validation warehouse.
4
Leg 1 — Seller Ships to Warehouse
Cabrella generates insured shipping label (Seller → Warehouse). Tracking accessible to buyer, seller, and admin in real time.
5
Warehouse Validation
Warehouse validates purity, weight, and authenticity. Admin updates validation status. Premium tier includes spectrographic analysis report.
6
Leg 2 — Warehouse Ships to Buyer
Cabrella generates second insured label (Warehouse → Buyer). Escrow released to seller via Stripe Connect payout upon confirmed delivery.
